Barrell Economic Project

"A home for Everyone!"

About 30 million car tyres are thrown away every year in Kenya. They end up in landfills where they release toxic chemical as time passes. These leads to pollution of environment especially water pollution. The company is committed to social responsibility.We are hand crafted by local artisan and the company is committed to a safe,healthy and supportive work environment. Overcrowded landfills can't handle the million of tyres that are thrown away each year. Instead some people take to illegally dumping their old tyres,and they tend to do so in low-income neighbourhood creating more and environmental and health risks for those who are already more to them.But here is the good news!Car tyres are actually recycled. Instead of burning we use them to make furniture and bag.We also make shoes which are later distributed to the beneficiaries who are homeless and also in school to needy children.

I intend to produce different products where we will sell our products in different areas. I also intend to make products such as furniture and bags.The tyres are then also used to make shoes which can be later distributed to needy children.

My Mission

"My Desire, Towards My Community"

My mission is to reduce environmental and water pollution caused by disposal scrap car tyres and make products through them.
